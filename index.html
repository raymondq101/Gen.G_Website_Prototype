03 — THE ORG
BRIDGING SEOUL + LA SINCE 2017.

Founded in 2017 as KSV eSports by Kabam co-founders Kevin Chou and Kent Wakeford, Gen.G — short for Generation Gaming — rebranded in May 2018 and inherited the Samsung Galaxy legacy: two League of Legends World Championships and one of the most decorated lineages in Korean esports.

2017 — THE DYNASTY ERA
Launched with the Seoul Dynasty Overwatch League franchise, then acquired the Samsung Galaxy LoL roster fresh off its 2017 Worlds title.
2024 — THE GOLDEN RUN
Four consecutive LCK championships, the 2024 MSI title, and a VALORANT Masters Shanghai trophy — all in one stretch.
BEYOND THE STAGE
Gen.G Global Academy develops the next generation across Korea and the US, with backing from LG, Monster Energy, and Logitech.
